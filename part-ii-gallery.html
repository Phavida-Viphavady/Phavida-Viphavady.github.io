Part II: The Lost Gallery

There are a total of 13 artworks missing from the Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum since 1990. These includes the works of Johannes Vermeer, Rembrandt van Rijn, Edgar Degas, Édouard Manet, Govaert Flinck and Pierre-Philippe Thomire.

What is left today are only the photograph of the pieces that we hope to be returned to the museum for people to admire in person.
